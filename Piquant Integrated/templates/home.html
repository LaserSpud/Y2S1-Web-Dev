Our chefs

Gordon Ramsay

Scotland-born Ramsay needs no introduction with his string of successful restaurants worldwide and appearances on TV shows such as Hell's Kitchen and MasterChef US. His namesake establishment, Restaurant Gordon Ramsay, has held three Michelin stars since 2001.

The highly acclaimed chef is also the author of a number of bestselling books, including his autobiography. Gordon Ramsay was also very famous for roasting people in Hell's Kitchen.

Jamie Oliver

Jamie Oliver is a British chef and restaurateur. He is known for his approachable and healthy cuisine, which has led him to front numerous television shows.

In 2005, he opened a campaign, Feed Me Better, to introduce schoolchildren to healthier foods, which was later backed by the government.
