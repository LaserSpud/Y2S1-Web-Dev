The restaurant

Located in the heart of Singapore on the crowded streets of Orchard, Piquant serves authentic and affordable food.

Piquant is a fine dining establishment that was created through hardwork by group members, Ian Sim, Joel Chan, Ernest Yan, Akif and Zhi Yang.

Serving many different types of food,the kitchen draws its ideas from cultures in many different countries such as,China,Russia,India and more.

From Prata served with chicken curry to Bliny,the restaurants menu balances intense flavors with fresh local produce or ingredients from other countries.

Restaurant Piquant's menu is served alongside the finest selection of wine,popular local drinks and not to forget cocktails as well.


In 2021,Restaurant Piquant was awarded 2 michelin stars,making it one of the rare few restaurants in Singapore to be awarded 2 michelin stars.
